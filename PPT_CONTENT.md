## SLIDE 1: Title

**Medha**
*AI-Powered Career Development Platform for Developers*

"From Confused Learner → Career-Ready Developer"

---

## SLIDE 2: The Problem

### 80% of CS students feel lost after graduation

- 🔴 **No direction** — random YouTube tutorials, zero career clarity
- 🔴 **No structure** — 500+ free resources but no roadmap
- 🔴 **No proof** — empty portfolios, generic resumes
- 🔴 **No community** — learning alone = 70% dropout rate
- 🔴 **No career link** — learning ≠ employability

> "I watched 200 hours of tutorials but can't build anything or land a job."

---

## SLIDE 3: Our Solution

### Medha = AI Career Coach + Learning Platform + Dev Community

| Pillar | How |
|--------|-----|
| 🤖 AI Career Guidance | Personalized roadmaps from real job market data |
| 📚 Structured Learning | Curated paths with checkpoints, not random videos |
| 🎮 Gamification | XP, ranks (Intern→CTO), 25+ achievements |
| 🏗️ Real Projects | Collaborate with peers, build portfolio-worthy work |
| 💬 Community | Native forum with voting, code reviews, discussions |
| 📊 Proof of Skills | Certificates, analytics, public developer profiles |

---

## SLIDE 4: How It Works

**Step 1:** Set your career goal → "I want to become a Full-Stack Developer"

**Step 2:** Get AI roadmap → Personalized learning path with resources & timelines

**Step 3:** Learn + Build + Collaborate → Projects, code reviews, forum discussions

**Step 4:** Prove your skills → Certificates, portfolio, career readiness score

## SLIDE 6: AI + Gamification Engine

### AI Intelligence
- **Groq LLM** analyzes real job postings (salary, skills, demand)
- **AI Mentor** knows your skills, rank, and goals — not generic advice
- **Video Quality AI** scores tutorials before you waste time

### Gamification
- **Career Ranks:** Intern → Junior → Developer → Senior → Lead → Architect → CTO
- **XP System:** Every action earns XP (projects +200, reviews +30, posts +10)
- **25+ Achievements** across 7 categories
- **Daily Streaks** + Global Leaderboard
